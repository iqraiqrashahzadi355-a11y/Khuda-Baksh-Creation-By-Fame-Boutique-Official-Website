The Story of Khuda Baksh Creation

Multan's Timeless Artistry meets Fame Boutique's Vision

Born in the heart of Multan, Khuda Baksh Creation has always been more than just a fashion label. It is a preservation of heritage. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the intricate, hand-woven beauty of Southern Punjab to the global stage.

Partnering with Fame Boutique, we have scaled our commitment to quality, ensuring that every stitch tells a story of meticulous dedication. Our artisans are the pulse of our brand, practicing age-old techniques passed down through generations, now refined for the modern silhouette.
